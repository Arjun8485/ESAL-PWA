WHY US

The textile industry is known for its excessive waste, to help address this issue we follow Eco-friendly production processes. We promote the use of stock fabric and reuse leftover fabric from both our own production and that of other brands to make necessary items for our products, like complementary items such as scrunchies and masks or cloth for hang tags, instead of relying on paper or plastic. The rest of the unused stock is donated to the underpriveledged to use.

We believe in maintaining a healthy and energising working environment while providing training opportunities for skill development as well as gainful employment for the local population.​
We always strive to live up to the ethical standards & continuously improve our working conditions.

As a sustainable & socially responsible company, we like to partner with companies who are oriented to our ethos as we synergise working, to make them attuned to these beliefs while they work on our productions.

Benifits of India

Raw Materials
Richness in basic raw materials, India is the worlds largest cotton producer and second-largest exporter, along with being the second largest producer of synthetic and artificial yarn and fibers. India also produces substantial quantities of Silk, Jute, Linen and is an excellent producer of various leathers.​

Culture and heritage
India has a rich culture and heritage for textiles, our block print and tie-dye techniques are much sought after. Our unique printing and dyeing techniques reflecting vibrant colours and intricate design have been acquired and nurtured over centuries. i.e. Sanganeri Prints, Jodhpur Bandhni, Punjabi Kantha, Lucknow Broderies and Shantineketan Leather to name a few, have rich traditional allure.

Short Lead Times
Short production turn around due to raw material availability and dedicated skillsets for Apparel Export.

Skilled Labour
Qualified and well-trained manpower​.

Well-developed

State of the art cutting edge textile processing finishing industry geared to address small, medium and large volumes.

Policies

Liberal trade policies.
