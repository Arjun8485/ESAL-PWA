HOW WE WORK

Encore simplifies the production process for fashion brands across the world. We play the role of a mediator between our clients and the apparel manufacturers in India, by managing everything from merchandising to logistics for our buyers.

logistics

We take care of all correspondence and protocol for sample development and production manufacturing through resolution oriented, fast and accurate responses.

Our local reach, leverage, and decades of experience provide us with an extensive network of product suppliers ensuring that we are able to continually cater to the varied needs of our customers.
Currently we represent garment suppliers / factories, who specialize in the areas of :

Woven, Circular & Flat Knitted Apparel
Hand-made products
Accessories, Embroidery & Surface ornamentation
Fabric & Garments Printing
pparel and Accessories in real Leather.

Our team is trained to follow AQL 1.5 up to 4.0

We know that a good product will distinguish itself from an ordinary one only on the merit of its quality. Having worked in Quality Assurance for a world leader in infant body suits where quality could never be compromised, we understand the importance of exceptional quality. As a service provider we aim to distinguish ourselves from other providers on the basis of our dedication to quality in the most practical way.

Stringent ways are followed to avoid pilferage along the process of transaction of goods from factory to the buyer’s ware-house. Follow up until the documents / goods reach the buyer.

Shipping & logistical services include:

Specific packaging of goods
Appoint & Liaise with freight forwarding agencies
Addressing Custom clearance formalities
Conforming to Export / Import rules & guidelines
Handling all documentation for shipment of goods
Exploring the fastest cost-effective freight connections & couriers.
